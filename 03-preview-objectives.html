Learning Objectives

Three focus areas for this lesson: tools, hands-on practice, and ethical use.

Tool Identification

Identify and compare key platforms used in the course: DALL·E, Midjourney, Leonardo AI, Adobe Firefly, and Canva’s AI Image Generator.

Practical Application

Create original images from text prompts, iterate using prompt-engineering techniques, and publish results to the Padlet board.

Ethical Understanding

Apply copyright, privacy, and authenticity principles when producing and sharing generative imagery.
